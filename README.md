# American-Express-Credit-Card-Risks-Dashboard

# 💳 Credit Card Risk Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Credit Card Risk Analysis Dashboard** is a business intelligence project developed using **Power BI** to analyze customer spending behavior, credit risk indicators, delinquency trends, income distribution, and employment status.

This dashboard helps financial institutions identify high-risk customers, monitor repayment behavior, and generate actionable business insights for better credit risk management.

The project demonstrates practical data analytics, dashboard storytelling, KPI monitoring, and business intelligence skills.

---

# 🚀 Dashboard Preview

## Main Features:

* Total Spend Analysis
* Delinquency Risk Monitoring
* Spending Category Breakdown
* Credit Score Analysis
* Employment Status Analysis
* Income Bracket Distribution
* Interactive Date Filtering
* Business KPI Tracking

---

# 📊 Key Insights

* 💰 Total customer spending reached **12.42 Million**.
* 🎬 Entertainment category generated the highest spending.
* ⚠️ Most delinquent customers fall under **1–30 Days Past Due**.
* 👨‍💼 Employed customers represent the majority customer segment.
* 📈 Medium-income customers (50K–100K) dominate the dataset.
* 📉 Lower credit score groups show higher financial instability.
